Global StarCraft II League (GSL) is a StarCraft II: Heart of the Swarm tournament hosted by GOMeXp and Blizzard Entertainment in South Korea. GSL first came into existence around august 2010. There first StarCraft 2 tournament "GSL League 1" was a huge success in South Korea and was meet with great excitement. From them on you GSL tournaments carried a great prestige, and still do to this day. The format of GSL tournaments is rather long and hard, thus where it gets its prestige from. First there is Group Stage #1 (Round of 32), then, Group Stage #2 (Round of 16),during these group stages everyone plays everyone twice and if players are even on points so go into a head to head best of 3 where the winner advances to the next round, then, Quarter-finals: Single-elimination play-offs. After this it comes down to the semi-finals and Final which are played as best of 7 matches, that means you have to win 4 of the 7 matches to win. It would not be over the top to say that GSL truly is one of the premier Tournament hosts not just in Korea but the whole world.

The StarCraft II StarLeague is a large tournament-based League held on location in South Korea and holds multiple events each year. StarLeague have there events televised on South Korea national TC by SPOTV which allows them to garner a huge audience and has allowed them to quickly beocme a major player in the StarCraft 2 tournament scene along with GSL and the Kespa Cup. This League has only be going for two seasons the first of which started in December of 14 and finished February 2015. These tournaments have representatives from all the major Kespa teams such as, SK Telecom, KT Rolster, CJ entus and so on. The SSL is an elaborate tournament which is separated into two tiers. Main Event contains the top tier players who are competing against each other to win the SSL and challenge contains players who are competing against each other to enter the Main Event. Challenge format is matches are Best-of-five the winner of the Bo5 advance to the Group Stage. The loser of the Bo5 is eliminated and may qualify through next season's qualifiers. For the main-event there are 16 players in for groups of 4 who play in a double elimation format. After these they go into the playoffs, the quarter-finals are best-of-5 while the semi-finals and finals are best-of-7.
